## Summary

BrainDash is a fully offline collection of puzzle and logic games. **We do not
collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.** The app has no user
accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no advertising. Everything you do stays
on your device.

## Information we collect

**None that leaves your device.** BrainDash does not gather personal information
and does not send any data to us or to third parties. There is no backend server.

## Information stored locally on your device

To make the games work, the app saves the following **only on your device**
(via Android's local app storage / shared preferences). This data never leaves
the device and we cannot see it:

- Game progress: best scores, level completions, and star ratings.
- Progression: XP, level, daily streak, achievements, and unlocked cosmetic skins.
- Preferences: your chosen difficulty per game, sound on/off, and whether the
  daily reminder is enabled.

You can erase all of this at any time by using the in-app reset option or by
clearing the app's storage in **Android Settings → Apps → BrainDash → Storage**,
or by uninstalling the app.

## Permissions we request

- **Notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`)** — used only to show an optional local
  "daily reminder" that you turn on yourself in Settings. The reminder is
  generated on your device; no notification data is sent anywhere. You can turn
  it off in the app or in system settings at any time.
- **Run at startup / boot (`RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED`)** — used only so a daily
  reminder you have enabled is re-scheduled after your device restarts.

## Sharing your scores

If you tap "Share," the app lets **you** send a score image/text to another app
you choose (messaging, social, etc.) using the standard Android share sheet. This
happens only when you initiate it, and the content goes to the app you pick — not
to us. We have no involvement in or visibility of what you share.

## Third-party services

BrainDash includes **no** advertising SDKs, **no** analytics, and **no** crash
reporting. It makes no network requests of its own.

## Children's privacy

BrainDash does not collect any personal information from anyone, including
children. We do not knowingly collect data from children because we do not
collect data at all. _[If you target a child audience in the Play Console, also

## Data security

Because no data is transmitted off your device, there is no data in transit to
intercept. On-device data is protected by Android's standard app sandboxing.

## Your rights

Since we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or
delete on a server. You retain full control of all locally stored data and can
delete it as described above.
